Frequently Asked Questions

What are your business hours?

Our customer service team is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Pacific Standard Time. Technical support is available 24/7 for emergency issues with installed systems. Our showroom at our Portland headquarters is open to the public Monday through Saturday from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on major holidays including New Year's Day, Independence Day, Thanksgiving, and Christmas Day.

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a free consultation with our rainwater harvesting specialists in several ways:

  • Call our sales team at 1-800-RAIN-SALE (1-800-724-6378)
  • Email sales@raindrops.com with your preferred dates and times
  • Use the online scheduling tool in your customer portal (for registered users)
  • Visit our showroom during business hours

Consultations typically last 60-90 minutes and can be conducted in person, by phone, or via video conference. We recommend having information about your property available, including roof area, water usage needs, and any existing water collection systems.

What information should I have ready when contacting support?

To help us resolve your technical support inquiry as quickly as possible, please have the following information available:

  • Your RainDrops system model number (found on your installation documentation or equipment labels)
  • Date of installation
  • Description of the issue including when it started and any error messages
  • Photos or videos of the issue (if applicable)
  • Your account information (if you're a registered user)
  • Recent maintenance or changes to your system

For smart system issues, please note your app version number and mobile device operating system. Having this information ready will help our support team diagnose and resolve your issue more efficiently.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we provide emergency services for critical system failures that could lead to property damage or health hazards. Our 24/7 emergency line (1-800-RAIN-EMG) is staffed by trained technicians who can provide immediate assistance for:

  • Major leaks or flooding caused by system failures
  • Complete system shutdowns affecting critical water supply
  • Water quality issues posing health risks
  • Electrical or safety hazards related to system components

Emergency service calls may incur additional fees depending on the time and circumstances. Non-emergency issues reported through the emergency line will be redirected to our standard support channels during business hours.

How can I become a RainDrops dealer or installer?

We welcome inquiries from qualified professionals interested in joining our network of authorized dealers and installers. The application process includes:

  • Completion of our online dealer application form
  • Proof of relevant business licenses and insurance
  • Demonstrated experience in plumbing, water systems, or related fields
  • Completion of our certification training program
  • Agreement to our dealer terms and quality standards

Benefits of becoming an authorized RainDrops dealer include access to our premium products, technical training, marketing support, and lead generation. For more information, please contact our dealer network team at dealers@raindrops.com or call 1-800-RAIN-DLR (1-800-724-6357).

What is your warranty claim process?

To initiate a warranty claim, please follow these steps:

  1. Gather your proof of purchase and product serial numbers
  2. Document the issue with photos or videos if possible
  3. Contact our warranty department at warranty@raindrops.com or call 1-800-RAIN-WRN (1-800-724-6379)
  4. Our team will verify your warranty coverage and guide you through next steps
  5. For eligible claims, we will arrange for repair, replacement, or service as appropriate

Most warranty claims are processed within 5-7 business days. Some claims may require inspection by an authorized technician. Please note that warranty coverage does not include damage caused by improper installation, misuse, or natural disasters. Refer to your product documentation for specific warranty terms and limitations.

Do you offer volume discounts for large projects?

Yes, we offer customized pricing for large-scale projects including:

  • Multi-unit residential buildings
  • Commercial and industrial installations
  • Municipal water projects
  • Educational institution installations
  • Non-profit and community projects

Discounts are based on order volume, project scope, and partnership opportunities. Our commercial sales team can provide a customized quote based on your specific needs. Please contact our commercial division at commercial@raindrops.com or call 1-800-RAIN-COM (1-800-724-6266) to discuss your project requirements and potential savings.

How do I request a quote for a custom system?

To receive a quote for a custom rainwater harvesting system, please provide the following information to our design team:

  • Property address and site plans (if available)
  • Roof area and material details
  • Intended water uses (irrigation, toilet flushing, etc.)
  • Estimated daily water requirements
  • Local rainfall data (we can assist with this if needed)
  • Any special requirements or constraints

Our team will review your specifications and provide a detailed proposal typically within 7-10 business days. For complex projects, we may recommend a site visit (which may incur a fee that can be applied toward your system purchase if you proceed). Contact our custom solutions team at custom@raindrops.com to begin the quoting process.
