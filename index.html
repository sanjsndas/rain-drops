How RainDrops Works

Our four-step process makes rainwater harvesting simple and effective for urban environments.

1

Rain Capture

Specialized catchment surfaces collect rainwater from rooftops and other impervious surfaces.

2

Filtration

Multi-stage filtration removes debris and contaminants before storage.

3

Smart Storage

Water is stored in our insulated, UV-protected vaults with real-time monitoring.

4

Usage

Clean water is available for irrigation, cleaning, toilet flushing, and more.
